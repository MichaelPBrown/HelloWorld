The Seahawks in the future

Where will the Seahawks go

There are many places for the 'Hawks to go from their current postion. They have a good chance to go up and go undefeated next year an following. However, they could go down depending on player trades that have been made, there is a chance that they could stay the same though.

Where will the players end up

Many of the players have been traded or have left from the earlier teams. For example, the LOB have all disbanded and left the Seahawks, we have also lost Beastmode. However, we have players that will have to retire sometime soon due to age, such as Russell Wilson and Chris Carson.
