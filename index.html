A Brief History

The 'Hawks took the field for the first time in 1976, that marked the uprbrining of the north and west in the league, as of '76 it was a league rooted in the North-East and the Seahawks and other teams that followed spread the league across America.

However, to start that season, the Seahawks had to scramble to put a team and find a new head coach in time, which is when Jack Patera was brought in. Little did they know that Patera would be the best for them leading them to a 10-6 and 9-7 records and Patera was coach of the year both times.
